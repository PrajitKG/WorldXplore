Shibuya Sky

This stunning observation deck gives a bird’s eye view of Shibuya and its surrounding area. As a Singaporean, you’ll appreciate the panoramic views reminiscent of our cityscape. Whether you visit during the day or at night, the Shibuya Sky view is breathtaking.

Harajuku

Known for its quirky fashion and vibrant street culture, Harajuku is a must-visit neighborhood in Shibuya. Explore the narrow streets filled with unique shops, cafes, and restaurants. As a Singaporean, you’ll appreciate Harajuku's eclectic fashion choices and trendy vibes.

Meiji Shrine

Immerse yourself in Japan's rich history and spiritual traditions at the Meiji Shrine. This Shinto shrine, dedicated to Emperor Meiji and Empress Shoken, is a popular tourist destination. As a Singaporean, you’ll appreciate learning about Japanese culture and paying your respects at this sacred site.
